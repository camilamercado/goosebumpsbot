There's a flash of blue light.
When your eyes clear, you see that you, Wendy, and the genie
are sitting in a small, round room.
The walls are covered with striped silk. "Awesome!" you shout.
"You saved us! Now take me to my aunt and uncle."
"Sorry, no can do," the genie replies.
"But, my wish is your command", you protest.
"Only if you're on the carpet," he sneers.
You and Wendy search frantically. No carpet.
"You didn't say to bring the carpet someplace safe" -
"only the two of you," the genie tells you.
"Now I don't have to listen to you." But... "where are we?" Wendy demands.
"Someplace safe," the genie replies.
"We're in my bottle. And we'll stay here until someone else talks to that carpet.
It should only take two hundred years."
"Oh, no!" Wendy moans. "You got us trapped in this bottle!"
She's right, you realize. You're so mad at yourself, you want to burst.
Too bad. It looks like you're going to have to
keep your anger all bottled up for now.

THE END
